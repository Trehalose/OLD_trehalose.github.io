2017My Viking Story

Tools:

  • Javacript
  • React
  • CSS
  • Bootstrap
  • AdminLTE

About this project...

This is an interesting two-parter client project we had at my work, and was the first official project I started my job at Sourcetoad with. One was a consumer engagement widget where a user could submit photos from their device, Facebook, and Instagram to the client's marketing site and share the experience they had with our client's services. The other half of this was an administrative panel to help manage data sent to and from the widget and the pages it is used on. I worked specifically on the frontend of these two projects, adding everything from components to styling, API calling to automated user acceptance testing, and so-on and so-forth. Facebook and Instagram functionality has since been removed from this project, but device uploading is still in action!
